MEOM a computational oceanography research group at IGE

Our MultiscalE Ocean Modelling (MEOM) research group at Laboratoire de Glaciologie et Géophysique de l’Environnement (LGGE) promotes an integrated data-driven approach to computational oceanography that combines ocean modelling, geophysical fluid dynamics, and satellite/in-situ observations.
